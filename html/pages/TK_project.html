The Teaching Kitchen @ Stanford

The Teaching Kitchen @ Stanford is an educational cooking program for students that gives them the opportunity to learn the fundamentals of cooking delicious and healthy meals. Inspiration for the campaign and environment asthetics for the Teaching Kitchen is inspired by the partnership with acclaimed chef and food education advocate Jaime Oliver. By focusing on are influenced by the fresh and local ingredients, the diversity of visiting guest chefs and the excitment and vibrance that students and faculty bring with them to each class.
